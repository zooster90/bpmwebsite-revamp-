Welcome

Builtech Project Management Sdn Bhd is certified under ISO 9001, ISO 45001, and ISO 14001 management systems. Our scope of management systems covers provision of project management and construction services for building and civil engineering works. We have strong project teams, latest technologies, good construction practices and techniques in place. Our site safety and health, and environment aspects are well controlled, ensuring our workers and public’s safety with the well sustaining of green environment. We continuously improve and kaizen our work practices, working towards greater work efficiency and effectiveness to achieve greater customer satisfaction.

We value all types of business relationships, emphasize on win-win situation with our stakeholders, forming partnership with our valued subcontractors. We embrace the corporate culture of high business ethics and integrity. We ensure that our product comply to specifications, drawings, BQ, acts and regulations.

Our employees are viewed as members of the big family. We emphasize on work, life balancing and we share the fruits of our hard works by providing series of employee benefits as well as team building activities. We recognize our part in contributing back to our communities. Yearly, we plan on our Corporate Social Responsibility sponsorship and community service.

We monitor and evaluate on the quality of construction materials use and competencies of workers at site, research for better methodologies of works, meeting CONQUAS standards. We also engage people with different expertise at different area of activity at site to lead and guide in quality checking works at site and problem solving. We are committed to deliver the best in quality with value added services at competitive price.

Throughout the years in construction industry, we have expanded our company portfolio, market segments and the business scales, with good company reputation and image. We are with good past records in providing quality end product, good response time, good work progress, and good controls on our workers’ safety and health and green environment initiatives with hands on attitude.
